Chapter 1 About

This document contains write-ups and explanations on the Y platform, currently created by the social protocols organization.

At this point, Y has not been launched yet and is under rapid development, so this is a living document and the information in it may become outdated fast. Nonetheless, we try to maintain up-to-date explanations of our ideas here.

We will introduce the building blocks of our algorithms; simple, but important concepts which make up the foundation of the components from which the Y platform is built.
